Shawn Urban - Game Designer

My name is Shawn Urban. I am a Game Design & Development
student at Quinnipiac University.

I have been playing video games since I was a kid, and not
once did I find myself thinking about pursuing a different
career path. Once I got to college, I hit the ground
running. Being able to apply myself in a field that I love
has been the most enjoyable and empowering part of my
education.

I have gained invaluable knowledge about the process of design, programming,
graphics, and critical thinking. Problem solving on my own and in teams has
given me many opportunities to adapt to and experience varying work environments.
Embracing and working around limitations has given me new perspectives towards
design. Failures and successes in the creative process allow me to continue
expanding my perspective. I've learned to jump into every opportunity and to
never stop learning.
