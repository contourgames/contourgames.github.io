Shawn Urban - Game Designer

My name is Shawn Urban. I am a Game Design & Development
student at Quinnipiac University.

My hobbies include a love for photography and video games.

Through photography, I have gained a great understanding
of framing, composition, angles, and lighting. I can use
these skills to analyze the impact that these concepts
have on video games.


I plan to use what I learn about game design to build an in-depth understanding
of the development of video games.
My goal is to work with a team to produce games with a meaningful impact on
players.
