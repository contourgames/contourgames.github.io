This is the roof of a Mental Developmental Center. After 25 years of abandonment, it is still a popular spot for rebellious teenagers.


Amazing graffiti spanning across the supports of a small railroad. The texture of the cement is still clearly seen through the paint, giving the art a lot of character.


From the rooftops, the city life continues to move, in contrast to the still observers.


The city breathes it's final breath on the ones who look down upon it.


New perspectives form when focusing on the intricacies of smaller, organic objects, rather than giant, man-made ones.
These images focus on environmental details and exploration




At dusk, the town shops turn on their neon signs, creating awesome scenes and visual tones as the city-goers walk past.



The leading lines in this photo bring the viewer into the environment, attracting attention away from the subject. The distant lights draw the eyes down the bridge, following the subjects gaze.



25 years of abandonment has lead to abuse of this setting; arson fires have scorched the inside and collpased the ceiling. Plant life continues to grow after the attacks on their environment.
